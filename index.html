This Shark has the Stuff It Takes too Take Down a Surfer!

Friday May 29,2015

Sometimes it's Mary Lee. Other times it's Katharine. This time, according to OCEARCH, both great whites sharks recently checked in near the South Carolina coast.Katharine pinged in at 9:40 a.m. while Mary Lee last pinged in Thursday morning.That's exciting news for fans of the sharks, both of which have their own Facebook pages.

What A Big Mouth You Have!

Mary Lee, at 16-feet-long, 3,500 pounds (and nearly 32,000 Facebook fans) is still the big fish in town. But Katharine, a 14-foot, 2,300-pound great white has become more popular after multiple visits.

Both sharks were tagged by OCEARCH, a nonprofit organization studying the sharks.You can track all of the sharks here. They also do a great job posting updates on their Facebook page and Twitter account. Follow them to see if the sharks get any closer.
